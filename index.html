Our Story

A Local Favourite Since Day One

Nestled in the heart of Hyde, Tameside, The Village Bakery & Sandwich Shop has been crafting fresh, homemade bakes for the local community with warmth and care. Every morning, we wake before the sun to ensure our breads, cakes, pastries and sandwiches are made to the highest standard — just like they should be.

From flaky pastries to indulgent brownies, hearty sandwiches to celebration cakes — everything is made with love, locally sourced ingredients, and the honest belief that good food brings people together.
